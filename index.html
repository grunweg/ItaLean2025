ItaLean 2025

Bridging Formal Mathematics and AI

Bologna, Italy – | December 9-12, 2025

ItaLean 2025 brings together researchers and practitioners working on formalization of mathematics in Lean and the synergy between machine learning and formal methods. The program features invited lectures, hands-on tutorials, academic and industrial research talks, product demonstrations and a concluding panel discussion.

Participate

Applications to participate in ItaLean 2025 are now open. Priority will be given to applications submitted by October 31.

Participation is free of charge and includes access to all conference sessions, lunches, coffee breaks, and the social dinner. A limited amount of financial support for travel and accommodation may be available. Applicants may indicate their need for support in the application form.

Applicants are encouraged to include a CV with their submission. By applying, you consent to your CV being shared with our sponsors.
